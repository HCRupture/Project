South Korea

South Korea is a highly developed country with a high rank on the Human Development index (HDI). It's Citizens enjoy a good quality of life due in part to it's rapid growth as one of the largest economies in the world of the past several decades.

Originally as the Korean Empire was succeeded and annexed into the Empire of Japan in 1910 after Japan surrender in WW2 in 1945, Korea was divided into two zones. North and South. Both zones were occupied, the North being occupied by the Soviet Union and the South occupied by the United States. Negotiations for reunification have not pulled through to this day. Officially, South Korea is known as the Republic of Korea (ROK)
